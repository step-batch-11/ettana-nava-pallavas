ETTANA

The Looms Of Kanchi

Step into the world of traditional Indian handloom weaving, where strategy meets culture. In Ettana, players take on the role of skilled weavers, crafting intricate patterns using vibrant yarns while competing to earn victory points.

Plan your moves carefully, manipulate patterns, and outplay your opponents to become the master weaver. The first to reach 8 Annas wins.
